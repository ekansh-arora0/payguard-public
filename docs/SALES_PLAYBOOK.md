## SECTION 1: THE PROBLEM (use this in every conversation)

### The Data You Lead With

- **$12.5 billion** in consumer fraud losses in 2024 (FTC)
- **$2.77 billion** lost to Business Email Compromise alone (FBI, 2024)
- **1,265%** surge in phishing volume since AI tools became mainstream
- **703%** jump in credential phishing in H2 2024
- **$4.88 million** average cost of a breach (IBM, 2025)
- **74%** of healthcare breaches involve the human element (Verizon DBIR, 2025)
- **45%** of all health data breaches hit organizations under 100 employees (HHS OCR, 2025)

### The Problem Statement (30-second version)

> "AI-generated phishing is exploding. Your employees get hit every day with messages that look real — no typos, no grammar errors, cloned logos, personalized text. Your email filter catches maybe 60-70% of it. The rest lands in someone's inbox and your team has to hope they don't click. One wrong click costs $4.88 million on average. PayGuard stops that at the browser level — before your people even see the scam."

## SECTION 2: YOUR PITCH (60 seconds)

### Opening Hook

> "I'm not going to sell you antivirus. I'm going to show you something that catches what antivirus misses."

### Problem Agitation

> "Every week, one of your people gets a phishing email that looks perfect. It's got your bank's logo, your vendor's name, the right tone. Your email gateway didn't catch it. Your training says 'look for typos' — but AI doesn't make typos anymore. What if your system could catch those *before* someone clicks?"

### Solution

> "PayGuard is an AI-powered phishing detection layer that sits on top of your existing stack. It analyzes URLs, page content, text messages, and screenshots in real time using machine learning models trained on millions of phishing patterns. It gives a risk score in under 200 milliseconds. It's not replacing your email gateway — it's catching what your gateway misses."

### Proof Points

- XGBoost ML model trained on phishing URL patterns
- Multi-signal analysis: URL reputation feeds + HTML code inspection + text scam detection
- Works as API, browser extension, or desktop agent
- Sub-200ms response time
- 99.9% uptime SLA

### Close

> "I'd like to run a 30-day pilot on 10 of your endpoints. You'll see exactly what gets caught that your current tools miss. No commitment. If it doesn't find threats your current stack misses, you walk away."
