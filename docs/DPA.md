# PayGuard Data Processing Addendum (DPA) - Draft

**Last Updated:** 2026-03-23

> Draft only. Obtain legal review before customer use.

## Scope
This DPA governs processing of Customer Personal Data under the main services agreement.

## Roles
- Customer: Controller
- PayGuard: Processor

## Processing Details
- Purpose: phishing/scam detection, abuse prevention, service reliability
- Data categories: URLs, threat metadata, operational logs (as configured)
- Duration: for the term of services plus retention window

## Security Measures
- Encryption in transit (TLS)
- Access controls and least privilege
- Audit logging and monitoring
- Backup and disaster recovery controls

## Subprocessors
Maintain and publish subprocessor list and update-notice process.

## Breach Notification
Notify Customer without undue delay and provide known details, impact, and mitigation plan.

## Data Subject Rights
Assist Customer with access/deletion/correction requests where applicable.

## Return/Deletion
On termination, return or delete Customer data per agreement and legal requirements.
