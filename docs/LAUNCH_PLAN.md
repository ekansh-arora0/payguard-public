# PayGuard Step-by-Step Launch Plan
## Targeting Elders, MSPs, Credit Unions, Healthcare + VC Decision

---

## SHOULD YOU PITCH VCs?

**Short answer: Not yet. Here's why and when:**

### Why NOT now (month 0-3):
- VCs want traction metrics (MRR, users, growth rate) — you have none yet
- Pitching without revenue = weak negotiating position = bad deal
- You'll spend 2-3 months fundraising instead of selling
- Pre-revenue valuations are brutally low right now (2026 market)

### Why YES later (month 4-6):
- Once you have $5K-10K MRR, you can raise on your terms
- MSP contracts = predictable recurring revenue (VCs love this)
- Elder care angle = massive TAM ($300B+ senior living market)
- You'll have case studies and real data to show

### The plan:
1. **Month 1-3**: Bootstrap. Sell to MSPs, CUs, elder care. No VC.
2. **Month 4-6**: Get to $5K-10K MRR. Build case studies.
3. **Month 6-9**: Raise seed round ($500K-$1.5M) if you want to scale faster
4. **OR**: Keep bootstrapping. $10K MRR = $120K/year. That's a real business.

### What to prepare for VC (month 3-4):
- [ ] Revenue metrics (MRR, growth rate, churn)
- [ ] 3-5 case studies with real numbers
- [ ] TAM/SAM/SOM analysis (senior living = $300B market)
- [ ] Product demo video
- [ ] Team pitch (even if it's just you)

## STEP 2: CHROME EXTENSION (Week 2-3)

### What:
Browser extension that auto-protects while browsing.

### Why:
- Free installs → users experience protection → convert to paid
- Chrome Web Store drives traffic without marketing
- Reviews compound over time
- This is the #1 passive acquisition channel

### How:
- Port detection logic to Chrome extension (manifest v3)
- Show green/red shield in toolbar
- URL analysis on every navigation
- Alert popup when phishing detected
- Free tier: URL-only analysis
- Paid tier: full HTML + JS analysis

### Timeline: 1 week to build, 2 days to publish

## VC DECISION MATRIX

| MRR at Month 6 | Decision |
|----------------|----------|
| $0-2K | Keep bootstrapping. Product needs more work. |
| $2K-5K | Consider angels. $100-200K to hire salesperson. |
| $5K-10K | Pitch seed round. $500K-1.5M. You have traction. |
| $10K+ | You might not need VC. Consider keeping 100% ownership. |

### If you DO raise:
- Target: $500K-1.5M seed round
- Use: Hire 2 salespeople + 1 engineer + marketing
- Valuation: 10-15x MRR = $60K-150K at $5K MRR (realistic)
- Don't raise more than you need. Dilution kills.
