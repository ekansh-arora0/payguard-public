# PayGuard Service Level Agreement (SLA) - Draft

**Last Updated:** 2026-03-23

> Draft template; finalize commercially and legally.

## Availability Target
- Monthly uptime target: 99.9% (production API endpoints)

## Planned Maintenance
- Announced in advance where feasible.

## Severity Levels
- Sev1: full outage / critical security incident
- Sev2: major degradation
- Sev3: minor degradation

## Response Targets
- Sev1: acknowledge within 1 hour
- Sev2: acknowledge within 4 hours
- Sev3: next business day

## Service Credits (Example)
- <99.9% and >=99.0%: 10% monthly credit
- <99.0%: 25% monthly credit

## Exclusions
Credits do not apply to force majeure, customer misconfiguration, or third-party outages outside PayGuard control.
