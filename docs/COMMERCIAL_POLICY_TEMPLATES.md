# PayGuard Commercial Policy Templates (Starter)

Use this as a starting point with legal counsel before public launch.

## Terms of Service (ToS) - Required Sections

1. Service description and scope
2. Acceptable use and prohibited use
3. No guarantee of complete threat prevention
4. Limitation of liability
5. Indemnification
6. Termination and suspension
7. Governing law and dispute process

## Privacy Policy - Required Sections

1. What data is collected (telemetry, logs, metadata)
2. Why data is collected (security analytics, support)
3. Retention periods
4. Subprocessors/infrastructure providers
5. User rights (access, deletion, correction)
6. Contact for privacy requests

## Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

1. Controller/processor definitions
2. Processing instructions and purpose
3. Security measures (encryption, access control)
4. Subprocessor list and notification
5. Cross-border transfers
6. Breach notification timeline
7. Data return/deletion at contract end

## Service Level Agreement (SLA)

1. Uptime commitment (e.g., 99.9%)
2. Maintenance windows
3. Incident severity levels and response times
4. Service credits
5. Exclusions and force majeure

## Incident Response Policy (IRP)

1. Detection and triage
2. Containment and eradication
3. Communication plan (customers, regulators)
4. Postmortem and remediation tracking
